# Spatial-Autocorrelation-Analysis
Spatial Autocorrelation Analysis on New York

## Our Area of Interest and Data
Our area of interest is the entirety of New York State. The boundary dataset shows NY counties and our attribute dataset has many variables, but we will be focusing on the unemployment rate in 2023 (Unemployment_rate_2023). The shared geographic identifier used to join them will be the FIPS. New York has a state FIPS of 36.

The counties shapefile can be found at https://hub.arcgis.com/datasets/SyrUniv::counties-ny-st-/about, while the attribute dataset can be found at https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/county-level-data-sets/county-level-data-sets-download-data
